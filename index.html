Privacy Policy for KICK THE NIC

Kick the Nic is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled securely and responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We collect your name, email address, and a unique user ID through Firebase Authentication when you sign up for an account.
  • Usage Data: We use Firebase Analytics to track anonymized data about how users interact with the app, such as the screens they view and how often certain features are used. This data does not identify you personally.
  • Health-Related Data: The app allows you to manually enter information about your nicotine use, such as daily puffs, quit plans, and progress tracking. This data is entirely private and stored securely in Firestore.

2. How We Collect Data

  • User Input: During the onboarding process, users manually enter their personal and health-related data.
  • Automated Tracking: Firebase Analytics automatically tracks anonymized usage data to help us improve the app experience.

3. How We Store and Protect Your Data

  • Storage: All data is securely stored in Firestore, a database provided by Firebase, which ensures high standards of data security.
  • Protection: We use Firebase Authentication to protect your account and prevent unauthorized access. Only authenticated users can access their data.
  • Encryption: Data stored in Firestore is encrypted both at rest and in transit.

4. Do We Share Your Data?

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise share your personal or health-related data with any third parties.

We use Firebase Analytics for internal app performance tracking. All data collected by Firebase Analytics is anonymized and cannot be linked back to your personal information.

5. How Long Do We Retain Your Data?

We retain your data until you delete the app or cancel your subscription. You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time through the app.

6. How Can You Manage or Delete Your Data?

Users can delete their accounts and all associated data from within the app at any time. This will remove all personal and health-related information from our systems.

7. Does the App Use Tracking Technologies?

The app does not use cookies or any other tracking technologies across devices or sessions. Data is only tracked and collected within the app through Firebase Analytics for anonymized usage insights.

8. How Do We Handle Minors' Data?

Kick the Nic is available only to users aged 17 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under this age limit.

9. Compliance with Regulations

We comply with standard data protection laws and regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in Europe and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA) or California, you have specific rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete your data.

Kick the Nic is designed to help users quit vaping by tracking nicotine usage and encouraging healthier habits. All data entered by users, including daily puffs and quit plan progress, remains private and secure.
